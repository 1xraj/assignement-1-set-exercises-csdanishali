Delicious Vanilla Sponge Cake

Looking for a quick, fluffy, and tasty treat? This easy vanilla sponge cake recipe is a go-to dessert that requires minimal ingredients and time. Perfect for satisfying sweet cravings with minimal effort!

What You'll Need

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking soda

How to Make It

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter with the sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract, then slowly incorporate the dry ingredients.
  5. Mix in the milk and baking soda until well blended.
  6. Transfer the batter to a greased 9×9-inch baking pan.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before moving it to a wire rack to finish cooling.
